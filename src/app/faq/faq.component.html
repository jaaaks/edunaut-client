General

There are primarily 2 ways to bookmark a course:

  • From the listing page - To bookmark a course from the listing page, click on the “Bookmark” button which appears upon hovering over the course tile.
  • From the course content page - Click on the Bookmark button which is present at the top of every course page.
You can find all your bookmarked courses on your profile page under the ‘Bookmarks’ section. You can mark a course as ‘complete’ from the course’s description page and it will be added to your list of completed courses on the Profile Page. Apart from this, we also mark a course ‘complete’ after you review it.

Every course listed on our platform has its own description page (we call it the “course description page” or “description page”, in short). Here, learners can find all the details related to the course, including what reviews it has received on our platform.

Any logged-in user can review a course from its description page. Adding a review does not have any prerequisites and so, feel free to write down your experience of the course. In case you do add a review, we will assume that you have completed the course, and therefore add it to your completed courses.

Before you compare multiple courses, you’d need to select the courses you wish to compare. You can select courses in 2 ways -
  • From the listing page - To select a course from the listing page, click on the “Compare” button which appears on hovering over the course tile.
  • From the course content page - Click on the “Compare” button which is present at the top of every course page.
After you have selected the courses you wish to compare, just click on the “Compare” button on the compare tray, and voila, this is done!

Report

We encourage learners to report a review in case they violate our Content Policy or Review Guidelines. To report a course review, follow these steps:

  1. Click on three vertical dots at the top right corner of the review box and select “Report”.
  2. Select the category of the breach (and give an explanation, if needed).
  3. Click on “Submit Report” once you are done.

We only allow signed-in users to report a review so that we can assign reward points. These reward points, in turn, are used to assign badges to learners.

To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the “Report” on the top-right corner of every page.
  2. Select the nature of the breach and mention which fields are affected.
  3. Click on “Submit Report” once you are done.
