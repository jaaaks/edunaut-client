Policy Page
Edunaut is an online platform dedicated to helping learners discover courses, collaborate on projects, and create learning pathways. Our community is diverse and passionate, with different learners having different points of view. While participating, it’s important to be respectful of other learners.
To help us maintain our platform’s integrity, please ensure that your content is -
  • Friendly
    • We value critical dialogue. Disagree, but do so with credible references to back up your claim and do not attack other learners for their opinions. Hate speech is prohibited.
  • Non-commercial
    • Spam and self-promotional content are prohibited. We reserve the right to remove any URLs from the comments.
  • Respectful of privacy
    • Content containing personal financial information including Credit Card details, Loyalty Rewards numbers, or other financial information can be removed. We allow multiple people to log-in using a single account (and therefore, share passwords).
  • Original
    • Any content plagiarized from other websites, reviewers, emails, or printed materials will be removed.
Edunaut reserves the right to remove any content which violates our rules. Please keep in mind the spirit in which these were written, and know that looking for loopholes is a waste of time.
*Individual communities on Edunaut may have their own rules in addition to ours and their moderators to enforce them.
