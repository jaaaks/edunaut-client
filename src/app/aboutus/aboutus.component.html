About Us

Who we are

Edunaut is an online platform dedicated to helping learners discover courses. collaborate on projects and create learning pathways.It was launched in September 2020, by students of IIM Amritsar, IIT Guwahati and IIT Roorkee.

What We Stand For

Our Vision

To add value to the learning ecosystem by helping users take the right course from an infinite pool of resources in the most efficient way.

Our Missions

Exhaustiveness- To create an exhaustive repository of online resources.
Evaluatory- To empower learners with tools to evaluate courses.
Unbiased- To ensure our course recommendations and rankings remain unbiased.
Discoverability-To expose learners to informative, yet under-appreciated courses.
Community Friendly- To empower users to build a community for learning.
Centralized- To help learners track the progress of all their courses at a single place.
Incremental- To provide user insights to our collaborators for creating the required course.
