About Us

Who we are

Edunaut is an online platform dedicated to helping learners discoveer courses. collaborate on projects and create learning pathways.It was launched in October 2020, by students and alumni of IIM Amritsar, IIT Guwahati and IIT Roorkee.

What We Stand For

Our Vision

To add value to the learning ecosystem by helping users find their perfect course from an infinite pool of online resources in the most efficient way.

Our Missions

Exhaustiveness- To create an exhaustive repository of online resources.
Evaluatory- To provide tools that empower learners to evaluate and chosse the right course.
Unbiased- To ensure our course recommendations and rankings remain unbiased.
Discoverability-To expose learners to informative, yet overlooked resources.
Community-friendly- To empower users to build a community for learning.
Centralized- To help learners track the progress of all their courses at a single place.
Incremental- To provide learners' insights to institutes for creating the required course.
